## Overview

Marketplaces like **Amazon** and **Flipkart** dominate **60%** of online retail sales in India, offering massive customer reach. However, this reach comes at a significant cost:

* **High Commissions:** Platforms typically charge a **10-15% commission** per sale.
* **Margin Erosion:** These high commissions heavily impact D2C brand margins and profit.

---

## Brand Dilution & The Loyalty Gap

**Over-reliance** on these platforms creates major long-term challenges for Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) brands:

* **Brand Identity Dilution:** The marketplace environment makes it difficult for D2C brands to build a unique, direct consumer connection.
* **Sacrificed Loyalty:** Brands struggle to foster **brand loyalty** and ownership of the customer relationship.

---

## ðŸŽ¯ The Critical Shift for D2C Success

To secure long-term profitability and brand strength, D2C companies must strategically work to **shift customers to their own websites**. Brands that fail to make this transition risk sacrificing both profit and brand loyalty to the platform giants.

* **Goal:** Maximize direct-to-consumer sales.
* **Result of Failure:** Loss of profit and permanent brand weakening.
